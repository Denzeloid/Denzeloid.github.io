Denzeloid's Gallery






City of Liyue

This photo is taken from one of the balconies of the Xinyue Kiosk in the inner city of Liyue. The shot features the Ministry of Civil Affairs where the Liyue Qixing and some Millelith troops reside.

Heyu Tea House

A shot in one of the seats in the Heyu Tea House. This establishment is one of the common spots for literature enthusiasts, as Yunjin and other performers express their artform through dancing, singing, and acting.




Chinese Guardian Dragon

Liyue, being a nation built from the culture of China, also has sculptures that are similar to statues in real-life. This stone idol can be seen in front of the Ministy of Civil Affairs

Stores In The Harbor

The docks in Liyue has two stories. The floor below has these simple stalls where vendors sell items like ores, food, artifacts, jars, cookware, and clothing.




Adepti's Tea Table

This abandoned tea set can be found in Mt. Aocang. Legend says that Mountain Carver and the Cloud Retainer, along with other Adeptus, would spend their time resting here.

Loading Dock

When the game first released, the people of Liyue still used this dock to construct their grandest boat ever, the Pearl Galley. Now, the extravagant boat can be seen sailing not too far from the harbor.




Baiju Guesthouse

This guesthouse has no interior that can be accessed by the player. But, the Wangshu Inn Bulletin Board says that a "prince" has booked a room in the inn.

Liyue Market

Liyue is well-known for its cuisine and high-quality ingredients. This market is right in front of the dock, so many seafood like crabs, shrimp, and fish are usually sold here.
